- Open chrome browser and go to extensions.
- Enable developer mode.
- Click on load unpacked.
- Select the "MyAdBlocker" folder
- Toggle the estensions.
- Start browsing. 
-If you encounter any ad, right-click on the ad and the click on "Block this ad" .  The link to the ad's website is then listed in the list of blocked sites.
-Right-click on the AdBlocker extension and choose options to manage the list of blocked sites.
